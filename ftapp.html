How can we better help our trainers?

A Mobile App Solution

The first round mockups can be found at Invision Mockups

Sincer our current FitnessTrainer is functional, our team wanted to explore the possibilities that a mobile app could offer, particularly for the trainers that find and manage clients through our service. These screens and mockups are purely my own work and ideas that I have played around with in my spare time; however, I believe that they offer a solution for trainers to be able to better manage their time and clients. Ideally this project needs an extensive design research portion using Human Centered Design methods to better understand the needs of our trainers and how we can create a better experience for them.

FitnessTrainer Currently

The website currently allows both users and trainers to have accounts, with trainers having an extensive back-end once logged in. Through their back-end, they can schedule training sessions, view client's info, and check each client's payment credits. However, what we currently have live is not yet mobile-friendly (though that is thankfully in the works). Creating a mobile app more suited specifically to trainers' needs would help allieviate some of the difficulties when having to use the website on their mobile browsers.
