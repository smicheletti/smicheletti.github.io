How can we better help our trainers?

A Mobile App Solution

Sincer our current FitnessTrainer is functional, our team wanted to explore the possibilities that a mobile app could offer, particularly for the trainers that find and manage clients through our service. Since the beginning of October, I have undertaken this project and finished an extensive research phase utilizing human centered design practices. Currently I am working on extremely low-fidelitiy prototypes by sketching out screens and the user flow by hand. Below are some quick images of the research and ideation process; if you would like to discuss the specifics further, I would love to do so in person.

FitnessTrainer

The website currently allows both users and trainers to have accounts, with trainers having a minimal back-end once logged in. Through their back-end, they can schedule training sessions, view client's info, and check each client's payment credits. However, what we currently have live is not yet mobile-friendly (though that is thankfully in the works). Creating a mobile app more suited specifically to trainers' needs would help allieviate some of the difficulties when having to use the website on their mobile browsers.
