Since his passing in 2013, the Serie Project has continued to keep alive his vision through advocating awareness of cultural diversity in the Austin art community through advancing the fine art of serigraphy.

Revitalizing the Project

In order to continue being a presence, the Serie Project has begun to undergo a revitalization. With a multitude of prints on hand, Sam's wife Jill Ramirez hopes to sell them to institutions and collectors across the country in order to fund the redo of the studio space (for updates view Serie Gallery Space WIP). In order to do this, I am in the process of migrating their website from their outdated WordPress to a new SquareSpace site.

New Website - Work In Progress

The Serie Project's WordPress website was in store for some much needed design changes to make it more user friendly, mobile optimization, as well as to better suit it for its new main purpose: selling and distributing the serigraph prints. Since I won't always be around to make changes or manage orders, I chose SquareSpace because of its great user interface which will make it much easier for Jill and her co-worker Linda to manage in the future.
