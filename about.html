ABOUT CONTACT

DESIGNER + PLANT LOVER

When it comes to design, or anything I touch for that matter, the most rewarding outcome lies in improving someone's life and while hopefully simultaneosly bringing them hapiness.

Thus far I have worked mainly in digital design along with some print work, but I have realized that is only the tip of the iceberg when it comes to design. When passionate, the perfectionist in me comes out, but also my more creative wild side.

Currently living in Austin, I've spent the majority of the past year working with a local startup, while collecting plants and figuring out what to do with my life. During the rare moments I'm not at work or stuck at a coffee shop working on freelance jobs, I enjoy exploring new restaurants, fawning over other people's dogs, and playing board games.
