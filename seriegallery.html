The Serie Project

Sam Coronadoâ€™s intention for the Serie Project was to create a solid platform that allowed for both established and emerging Latino artists to reach a bigger audience, and in turn, empower their words and creativity to spread. Since his passing in 2013, the Serie Project has continued to keep alive his vision through advocating awareness of cultural diversity in the Austin art community through advancing the fine art of serigraphy.

The Serie Project was recently awarded grant money by the City of Austin to rennovate the studio space into a combibned gallery and community serigraphy learning center. In order to give back to the community, we hope to design a welcoming space to help share Sam's love of art and serigraphy with the surrounding community. The entire studio space will be redone and circulum established for community art classes.
