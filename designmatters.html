How can you convey how important design is?

Education Through Exhibition

Through this curation of small, seemingly insignificant kitchen objects, visitors of the exhibition are meant to come to a better understanding as to why the design of objects matters and how they can evaluate objects in their lives.

Show Catalogue

This catalogue documents the exhibition and doubles by giving visitors of the exhibit a take-away and better understanding of the curation and giving viewers unable to attend a detailed description and analysis of the curation of objects. Graphic elements, physical evaluation sheets, and a digital leader-board in the space prompt visitors to start considering what it means for something to be designed well.

View Catalogue PDF

Object Evaluation

Upon entering the exhibition, visitors would each be given a copy of the catalogue containing examples of design criteria, questionaires, and space for notes. This content is not meant to be the end-all and does not mean to be the only way of critque, but means to be a way to prompt thought and introduce new concepts.
