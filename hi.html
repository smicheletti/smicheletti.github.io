How can you let someone know you're open to friendship?

All Alone

Think back to a day you felt lonely. Think you're the only one feeling this way? You are not alone. Whether you feel this way now or have felt this way before, reach out to others, say hello or ask how their day is going. Hi is a proposal for the installation of pieces around campus that are intended to spark conversation through ice breaker prompts on lunch trays and bus windows as well as a universal symbol meant to convey openness to strangers.

Conversation

These conversation starters are meant to encourage college students to reach out to the person next to them. So many people go through their day without interacting with a single stranger. Sometimes a smile has the power to complete uplift someone. While it might not seem like much, you could make a day, even make a friend.

Final Proposal

At our final critique in November 2014, I presented my research and findings along with proposed solutions in the form of a compact booklet. As a continuation of this project, I am currently working on refining the symbology.
