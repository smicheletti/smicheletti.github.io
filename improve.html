My Current Role

Designer: May 2015 - Present
Design Intern: Spring Semester 2015

Currently working as the sole designer for both MyTennisLessons and FitnessTrainer (MTL & FT), I have taken on the roles of UX/UI as well as front-end development and the occassional graphic design based projects. Throughout my time, I have successfully updated many pages on both websites in order to increase conversion rates as well as engagment while lowering the bounce rate.

MyTennisLessons.com

Specifically for MTL, I have recently launched successful SumoMe and MailChimp campaigns to capture customers would otherwise would have abandoned the site, successfully raising revenue about $7,000 in less than one month. SumoMe and MailChimp are common tools, but custom design imagery as well as precisely worded messages and incentives make all the difference.

FitnessTrainer.com

Coming in as an intern soon after the birth of FitnessTrainer, I was able to be much more involved in the design and functionality of the site as opposed to the already well established MTL site. I successfully raised the conversion rate of trainer signups by redesigning the sign up form, and we have continued to work on making the larger application more user-friendly. I was also instrumental in the redesign of the instructors' profile pages as well as the unqiue landing pages.

Iconography & Imagery

One of the things I have enjoyed greatly has been the creation of unique icons and imagery for both websites. Illustrator remains by far my strong suit in terms of the Adobe suite, and I have had ample opportunities to explore vector creation throughout my time with MTL and FT.
